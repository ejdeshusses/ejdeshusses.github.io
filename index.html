Improving an Argan Oil Production Process
Interdisciplinary Qualifying Project, Israel Jan. - Mar. 2022
  • Conducted research and proposed improvements to the sponsoring organization with a peer in Robotics.
  • Measured and analyzed cycle times, throughput, defects, and capabilities of the production process.
  • Designed and implemented a prototype using SolidWorks to minimize variation and increase batch sizes.
  • Standardized the process, despite the variation in argon nut size, shape, and kernel number.
Evaluating the Economic Feasibility and Design of Modular Production Systems
WPI Aug. 2022 - Mar. 2023
  • Collaborated with three students and an industry sponsor to assess the feasibility of fully modular production systems (MPS) in the steel industry. Indicators included ROI and profit margin.
  • Designed an abstract MPS, including process flow, cell layouts, cell-switching concepts, and PFMEA.
  • Analyzed the MPS with Monte Carlo Simulations, considering uncertainty and economic risks.
  • Designed an LP model to maximize profits by tailoring production to be resilient in economic turmoil.
Improving the WPI COVID Testing Process
Achieving Effective Operations (BUS 3020), WPI Aug. - Oct. 2020
  • Collaborated with three students to minimize wait times and costs of the on-campus testing process.
  • Analyzed the current process through KPIs, Value Stream Mapping (VSM), root-cause analysis, etc.
  • Applied the DMAIC process. Our proposed, and later implemented, solution saved at least $5k/month and cut wait times from 20-40 minutes to 1-1.5 minutes.
